About Mondson Trucking

Commitment to Excellence

Mondson Trucking, a proud family-owned business based in Georgia, is led by Mr. Setrol Mondson. Specializing in OTR (over-the-road) transportation, the company operates a fleet of 53-foot reefer units, ensuring that fresh freight reaches its destination with the highest standards of care. With a commitment to reliability and customer satisfaction, Mondson Trucking has become a trusted name in the industry, delivering quality service with a personal touch. Whether it’s produce or perishable goods, the company is dedicated to keeping America’s supply chain moving smoothly.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the leading trusted partner in fresh freight transportation.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver fresh goods on time with reliability and exceptional service.
